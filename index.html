Oregon Produces Great Runners.

One of those runners is Steve Prefontaine

A legend is born.

Born in Coos Bay, Oregon in 1951, Steve became one of the most admired and inspirational figures in American distance running.

He could run a mile super fast!

Not just a mile. He could run the mile, two-mile, and the three-mile incredibly fast. The average person can run a mile between seven and nine minutes. Steve's fastest mile was run in 3:54.6! In fact, check out his personal bests:

Amazing.

Steve Prefontaine broke every American record between 2,000 meters and 10,000 meters.

He is among some of Oregon's best distance runners.

Other great Oregon distance runners include: Ian Dobson and Galen Rupp most recently.
